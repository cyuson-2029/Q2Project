MAIN MISSION

The main mission of this advocacy is to help the environment and the citizens. We plan to help the environment by making people care for it more and by making people take collective actions against environmental pollution including waste, water, and air pollution. This advocacy may help people by helping the mto become accountable, responsible, and attentive of their surroundings.
