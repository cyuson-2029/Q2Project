The first problem that people may encounter is that most individuals may oppose to change their behavior. They resist the change simply because using materials such as plastic would be inefficient to them. It would also lead to them feeling as though the rules are getting stricter, and that someone else who follows the rules better would clean their mess up. Overall, this problem can be counteracted by empathy and love for the environment, and awareness,

The second problem that people may encounter is the lack of support and funding from other citizens and the government. Some may see projects that counteract waste management as costly and time consuming, so some would rather not go through the trouble. A fix to this problem is to show people the benefits of waste management, encourage volunteerism, and collaborate with local governments.

The third and final problem that people may encounter is that indivuals may be too busy with their own situation thus leading to less time for activities such as community service. Each and every citizen has their own life, and that's why in order to solve this problem, proper scheduling and management must be implemented.
