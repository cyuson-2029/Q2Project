ABOUT THE ADVOCACY

1. This advocacy is about spreading awareness for the importance of Waste Management in order to protect our environent from danger such as Pollution and Climate Change. In this advocacy, we'll be tackling the goals and problems people may encounter in implementing Waste Management.

2. This advocacy is important because it highlights the need for action and how every citizen can contribute to saving our environment. Health hazards and pollution may be prevented which will lead to a safe life. It's not just managing waste, it's saving lives.

3. We aim to help people have consistent efforts recycle, reduce, reuse, and segregate waste, have more participation in environmental programs, and encourage responsibility. The result of this will be communities wherein the people learn to help both each other and the environment alike with responsible empathy.
